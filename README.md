# SELLOUT — build the label

**▶ Play now (free, in your browser):** https://expyt.github.io/sellout/

**💬 Join the community:** https://discord.gg/wDF5yzP52R

Start in your bedroom with a heat press and a dream. Design pieces, stockpile them in your vault, and drop capsules and seasonal collections into a living fashion industry — with rising and falling trends, five seasons, Fashion Weeks, resellers, rival brands with real personalities, and a community that remembers everything you release.

## What makes it tick

- **Design ≠ Release** — build a vault of designs (some go stale, rare ones become timeless), then curate them into capsules or full collections when the moment is right
- **A living industry** — 12 trends rise and fall with narrated reasons, customer tastes drift, seasons reshape demand, and six AI rivals (VOID, NOVA, KITSUNE, OUTLAW, OBSIDIAN, PAPER CROWN) act in character
- **The Fashion Calendar** — Fashion Weeks, Black Friday, design competitions, Year-End Awards; schedule launches weeks ahead with automatic teaser campaigns
- **Time slots** — three slots a week: design, market, or drop. You can't do everything
- **Consequences you can read** — every drop gets a critic review, a customer breakdown, and a factor-by-factor explanation of *why* it went that way
- **Your legacy** — a permanent archive, brand personality earned through behaviour, milestones, awards, and reinventions

## Tech

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, no frameworks, no build step, no server — saves live in your browser via localStorage (with export/import). Every version is playable straight from this repo via GitHub Pages.
